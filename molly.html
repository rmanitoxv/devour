Human

Failed stand-up comedian. Used her college fund to pay for the cult's robes.

Appearance

Molly is tall and slender with red hair.



Possessed

After banishing the goat demon Azazel from cult leader Anna Puerta, she was committed to a secret psychiatric hospital suffering manic delusions. There, she was subjected to barbaric, outdated shock treatments that ate away at her body and mind. Slowly remembering the summoning ritual she had seen during her time with the cult, Molly attempted to raise Azazel once again, successfully.

Appearance

Possessed Molly, like most of The Possessed, is considerably taller than her human counterpart, has elongated forearms with razor-sharp claws and spiky dark red hair. Her face is disfigured, with deep sunken eyes and always-present grotesque smile. She is wearing torn white blood-stained dress (most likely a patient's gown).
