Human

Expert linguist and translator of ancient texts. Secretly terrified of cats.

Appearance

Zara is a short woman with a average body and black hair.



Possessed

Using rituals remembered from her time with satanic cult "The Watchers", Zara Yamashita has been attempting to summon the goat demon Azazel. Hidden away in the caves beneath a family owned inn, Zara discovered giant nesting spiders to offers up as foul blood sacrifices. But the ritual has gone wrong, and now Azazel has possessed Zara's body and mind in the cruellest of ways.

Appearance

Possessed Zara, unlike most of The Possessed, has average height but is slightly taller than her human counterpart. She'll transform into a giant spider once fully transformed. Her face is fully pale, with deep sunken eyes and has sharp teeth. She is wearing an old white kimono in her untransformed state (most likely a spa's robe).
